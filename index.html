About Tanupriya

Tanupriya is a self-taught hyperrealistic artist whose work blurs the boundary between drawing and photography. Working primarily in color pencil, graphite, and charcoal, she transforms blank paper into breathtaking portraits that seem to breathe.

Her subjects — from iconic pop-culture characters to the raw power of wildlife — are rendered with obsessive attention to texture, light, and emotion. Each piece can take hundreds of hours, built up layer by patient layer.

Based in India, Tanupriya shares her creative journey on Instagram as @tpgart, where her work has inspired thousands of fellow artists and art lovers around the world.
